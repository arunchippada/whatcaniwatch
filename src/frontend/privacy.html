Privacy Statement for WhatCanIWatch

The following statements describe the privacy practices for WhatCanIWatch

Personal User Information

WhatCanIWatch accesses your personal information from your facebook profile only in the context of your logged in session with WhatCanIWatch.

WhatCanIWatch does not persistently store your personal information on its servers nor does it share your personal information externally with other users or services.

Your personal information from your facebook profile is solely used for the purpose of delivering the service to you and only within your logged in session with WhatCanIWatch.

Log Files

WhatCanIWatch uses IP addresses to diagnose server problems, analyze trends, administer the site, and gather demographic information for aggregate use.

IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

WhatCanIWatch has standard security measures in place to protect the loss and misuse of sensitive information.

Cookies

WhatCanIWatch only uses cookies to maintain a logged in session across multiple requests from the same browser instance

WhatCanIWatch does not use cookies to track any other types of user behavior

Links

WhatCanIWatch contains links to other sites such as Facebook. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites. This privacy document applies only to WhatCanIWatch.

Contacting the Web Site

For any questions about this privacy statement, please contact: support@whatcaniwatch.com
